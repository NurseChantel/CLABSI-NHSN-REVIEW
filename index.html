Quick interpretation

For CLABSI attribution, an eligible central line must have been in place for more than two consecutive calendar days on the DOE or the day before.

This tool does not replace application of all NHSN LCBI criteria, including age-specific symptom rules, repeated organism requirements, or infection-window-period logic.
